# Linux Installation Centers (Linux ICs)

**Get help switching to Linux—or help others make the switch.**

Linux ICs connects people who want to install or try Linux with local experts who can guide them in person: choosing a distro, installation, and first steps—so you’re not on your own.

---

## For people who need Linux installation help

- **New to Linux?** Get matched with an expert near you for in-person or guided support.
- **Not sure which distro to pick?** Get advice tailored to your hardware and how you plan to use your computer.
- **Want someone to walk you through install?** Experts can help with installation, dual-boot, or trying Linux without removing your current system.
- **Support that fits you:** Options for free community help or paid sessions, depending on what you need.

**You’re in the right place if** you’re a student, professional, or enthusiast who’s interested in Linux but finds it overwhelming—we’re here to make the move easier.

---

## For experts who offer Linux support

- **Share your experience** and help newcomers in your area switch to Linux.
- **Set your own terms:** Offer free help, paid sessions, or both.
- **Get discovered** by people looking for local, human support—not just forums and wikis.
- **Grow the community** and turn your Linux knowledge into something that helps others (and can earn you income if you choose).

**You’re in the right place if** you already install or support Linux for friends, family, or colleagues and want a simple way to connect with more people who need that help.

---

## How it works

1. **Sign up** as someone who needs help or as an expert.
2. **Find a match**—by location and what kind of support you need or offer.
3. **Connect and get help** (or give it)—in person or in a way that works for both of you.

Linux ICs is built to make adopting Linux less confusing and more human—whether you’re the one switching or the one helping.
